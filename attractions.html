Bilbao is the city that offers you a lot of attraction. Some of them you can find on this page.



Public events




Concerts



Not only festivals, but also concerts are a perfect way to spend time. Famous performers from all over the world do not miss Bilbao to entertain fans with their new albums and compositions. They create new collaborations and make grandiose shows to satisfy fans. Sometimes you can come across two concerts in one day, but in no case you should miss the opportunity to attend such events.

Semana Grande (Big Week)



During the Semana Grande de Bilbao, there are fiestas in honour of the Virgen de Begoña. Bilbao is the setting for concerts, shows, theatre, fireworks and children's activities at different points in the city, such as Plaza Nueva square, Bizkaia, Unamuno and Encarnación squares, and the Urbitarte and Arenal wharves. The speeches and "chupinazo" (fireworks) to open the celebrations will take place from the Arriaga theatre. There will also be a programme of bullfights at the Vista Alegre bullring, including a horseback bullfight and eight normal bullfights.




Tours

There are some tours for you, if you like to get new knowledges about places where you go




Bilbao: The old town or "The Seven Streets" tour



This is the tour of the earliest heart of the city, constituted as a ville in the 14th century, talking about its origins and its evolution through the centuries.
The old part of the town has been transformed in a meeting point between local, bystanders and tourists who come to know the citty. The original Seven Streets, together with the new urbanistic routes which have been added over the course of the time, hosting its historic memory and Contemporary charm, a explosive mix which everybody likes because of its quirk.
Attraction is available in 2 languages (Spanish, English)
Price: 19.00 €/person

Highlights of Bilbao Bike Tour



Discover the city known for the Guggenheim Museum and its incomparable gastronomy.
The best way to see the best of Bilbao is on a bicycle tour. In just three hours you will see all the highlights of the city with an expert local guide. You can feel free to ask everything you want to know about the city, for instance where to get the best pintxos.
Attraction is available in 3 languages (Spanish, English, German)
Price: 32.00 €/person

Special Guggenheim Bilbao Tour



The tour includes an itinerary around the exterior area of the Guggenheim museum, from the side of the river walking close to museum, talking deeply about its architecture and artistic exterior works which have been added and form part of the exposition of the museum itself. Than we go deep in the museum where we explain the interior architecture and their collections, all of them told by an official Guggenheim Museum guide and member of BIlbaoguia.
Attraction is available in 2 languages (Spanish, English)
Price: 39.00 €

Basque Secrets: Chillida, Astigarraga and San Sebastian



This attraction gives opportunity to combining the art and the cider of the Basque Country! The way starts from going to Hernani, where you can hear about the life of the sculptor Eduardo Chilida, known for his works in iron and concerete. You will visit the Chilida-Leku Museum, surrounded by gardens where the artist's works are. Then you mowe to Astigarraga, a town famous for its Basque cider tradition. There you will have a tour to the Cider Museum to enjoy the culture of cider and to make testing through the rite. After one-hour lunch, you will continue to San Sebastian, where you will enjoy of guided tour of its Old Town and amagnificent panoramic view from Mount Igeldo.
Attraction is available in 2 languages s (Spanish, English)
Price: 75.00 €/person




Water

If you like ocean more than city view, there some attractions that connect with water.




Paragliding tandem flight in Sopelana



The best way to have an incredible journey and an unforgettable experience in the Basque Country. The beaches of Azkorri or Gorrondatxe are some of the wonders that you can observe from a privileged point of view. La Salvaje beach, in addition, is an ideal beach for flight, since the orientation of its slopes allows us to fly almost every day. It does not matter if the wind is easterly, northerly or westerly, the flight is guaranteed. From morning to sunset, the views will help you to disconnect and forget about everything else.
Price: 55.00 €/person | The price includes 20 minut of flight and video.

Surfing



Because of spectacular beaches, famous among surfers the world over, which are perfect for surfing and with a wide range of sea floors and breakers. Sopelana has a very consistent beach break waves that are available year round. There are many surf schools in the area. Suitable for beginners and intermediates. Sopelana is a clean sandy beach which is surrounded by beautiful cliffs. There is a nice café/bar just by the entrance which is worth a visit. Public toilets and showers are available on site.
Price: 70.00 €/ 2hours

Sailing Bilbao



Meeting with the skipper at the quay of the Maritime Museum to start a 2-hour crossing sailing through the old industrial area of Zorrozaurre and Shipyards, until reaching the Transporter Bridge of Bizkaia, World Heritage, and the residential area of Neguri and Las Arenas.
Return to Bilbao enjoying a wine tasting on board.
Attraction is available in 2 languages (Spanish, English)
Price: 75.00 €/person

Fishing day



Become a true sailor and embark aboard a ship equipped with the technology needed to enjoy an authentic journey on the high seas. You do not need to be an experienced fisherman, anyone can enjoy a fun day fishing. Outputs that are carried out in a motor boat where the pattern you will receive and will explain the operation of the same.
Finally, you will return to the port enjoying a tasting of txakoli on board and transfer to the hotel. Price reference based in 6 pax.
Attraction is available in 2 languages (Spanish, English)
Price: 255.00 €/6 people
